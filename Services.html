Our Services

Accounting

We prepare accounts for all types of business, from sole traders to partnerships and limited companies. We give you more time to concentrate on running your business, with peace of mind that your accounts and returns are in order.

Our accounting and compliance services include:

  • Preparation of annual accounts
  • Preparation of periodic management accounts
  • Maintaining PAYE and VAT records and associated returns
  • HMRC compliance and investigations
  • PAYE and National Insurance compliance
  • Self-assessment individual and company tax

Book-keeping

It's our job to get your books straight and keep them that way. We collect all receipts, bank statements and other papers and ensure they are filed correctly to meet HMRC requirements — all at a competitive price and at your convenience.

Tax Assistance

It really doesn't have to be taxing. We deal with HM Revenue & Customs on your behalf. With careful tax and financial planning, it may be possible to lower and defer the tax you pay, freeing up cash for other business and personal needs.

Payroll

Administering payroll is becoming increasingly burdensome for many businesses. Outsource your payroll to Afrax Accountant's specialists and free up time and resources to focus on your core business functions.

Business Start-up Consultation

Whether you are a new start-up or an established small business, we provide a personal and pro-active approach. We handle your accounting and tax matters (PAYE, VAT, Self-Assessment and Corporation Tax) and Companies House filings, so you can concentrate on running your business.
