About Us

Afrax Accounting is a professional accounting and tax advisory practice based in London, serving clients across the UK.

The firm has a policy of "No customer is too small" — we are very willing to assist anyone, from sole traders to partnerships, and from small firms to large limited companies.

Our clients are assured a high degree of quality service from our team of professional accountants and tax specialists.

As members of a national network of Afrax Accountants, we are able to call on the expertise and support of Chartered Certified Accountants and Tax Specialists, whilst remaining totally independent and offering a personal, friendly service without "Large Practice" fees.

We work within strict codes of professional conduct and competence.
